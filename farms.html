Colombia's Black Gold - A Colombian Coffee Documentary

Colombia's Black Gold is a Colombian Coffee Documentary that delves into the rich tapestry of Colombia's coffee production. From the lush landscapes of coffee plantations to the vibrant culture surrounding this beloved beverage, this film explores the intricate lives of the farmers and the indigenous communities that cultivate one of the world's finest coffees. Join us as we journey through the heart of Colombia's coffee region, highlighting the dedication, challenges, and pride of those who make it all possible.

Ethiopian Wild Coffee: Farmer and Forest-Friendly Beans

The Ethiopian Wild Coffee project supports farmers to protect forests and bring a unique coffee to global drinkers. Aiming to transform the wider forest coffee industry in Ethiopia, the initiative focuses on improving how wild-growing coffee is processed and marketing its forest-friendly credentials to roasters and buyers around the world. This video explores how coffee farmers, cooperatives, and roasters have worked to develop this new global value chain.
